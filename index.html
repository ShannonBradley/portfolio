About Me

After earning my bachelor’s degree from the University of Southern Indiana, I didn’t end up writing for a newspaper as I had planned. Now, many years later, I realize how fortunate I was to enter the world of corporate marketing right out of college. In my many marketing positions, I always used my writing skills, and that eventually led to my current position as a web and content manager as part of an internal digital marketing team. I use the Sitefinity content management system and digital experience platform, while working with an external development partner.

While stuck at home during the COVID-19 pandemic, I decided there was no better time to learn web development skills including HTML & CSS, Javascript and responsive design using Bootstrap. Realizing the possibilities driven by web development, and being able to create on my own, has given me a unique skillset and understanding of how to help a company be where the world is going.To learn more about me, visit my LinkedIn profile or download my resume.
